## We're Here to Help

If you're experiencing any issues with the 94Talent platform, have questions about your account, or need assistance with a hiring workflow, our support team is ready to help.

---

## Contact Us

The fastest way to get support is to email us directly. We aim to respond to all inquiries within **1 business day**.

<Card icon="envelope" href="mailto:support@94talent.com" title="Email Support">
  [support@94talent.com](mailto:support@94talent.com)
</Card>

When reaching out, it helps to include:

- A brief description of the issue you're experiencing
- The steps you took before the issue occurred
- Any error messages or screenshots if applicable
- Your account email address
